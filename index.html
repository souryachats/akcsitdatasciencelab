ABOUT US

Data Science is a concept to bundle Statistics, Computer Science and Domain Knowledge to analyse the actual phenomena of data. Fuelled by Big Data and AI, the demand of Data Science is growing exponentially and with this rise, the A.K CHOUDHURY SCHOOL OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (A.K.C.S.I.T). under University of Calcutta has also looked forward by opening a laboratory dedicated for exploring the world of Data Science. The data science laboratory has a Fuzitusu 16 core server with 256 GB RAM and a GPU will be added to the rack in the next 2 months. The lab has i5 machines with 16 GB RAM provided by TEQUIP and high-speed internet connectivity.
